About

Spookfest is a festival celebrating Halloween in the LQD High School organised by the English club The Talkaholic

Activities

You can Cosplay as your favourite, scary characters, meet all sorts of people and having fun playing minigames our club has to offer

Minigames include puzzle solving, riddles, pumpkin carving and many many more to keep you accompanied.
